Black Forest Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake :

Wheat Flour : 1 1/2 cups(180g)
Cocoa Powder : 1/2 cup (40g)
Baking Powder : 1 tsp
Baking Soda : 1/2 tsp
Salt : a pinch
Eggs : 4 large
Oil or Butter (melted) : 180ml
Powdered Sugar : 200g
Curd or sour cream : 1/2 cup (!20ml)
Vanilla essence : 1 tsp

For the Whipped Buttercream :

Unsalted Butter : 1 cup (225g)
Icing Sugar : 2 cups (250g)
Vanilla extract : 1 tsp

For Garnishing :

Grated chocolate (from a chocolate bar)
Cherries (as per your need)

Instructions

Step : 1

Mix Dry ingredients. In a large bowl, sift together
1 1/2 cups Wheat flour
1/2 cup cocoa Powder
1 tsp baking Powder
1/2 tsp baking Soda
A pinch of Salt
Mix well to combine the dry ingredients evenly, Set aside

Step : 2

Mix Wet Ingredients In another bowl, start by beating the 4 eggs. Then add : 3/4 cup of melter butter or oil 1 cup powdered Sugar

Beat the mixture using a hand mixer or whisk for about 1 min until well combined.

Next, add 1/2 cup curd or sour cream and beat again for 30 seconds to 1 min until smooth.

Step : 3

Combine Dry and Wet Ingredients.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in batches.
Mix gently after each addition to form a smooth, lump-free batter.

Once combined, add 1 tsp vanilla essence and mix until just incorporated.

Step : 4

Bake the Cake Grease two 7-8 inch round cake pans with butter or oil and dust with flour.

Pour the prepared batter evenly into the pans. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let the cakes cool completely on a wire rack before frosting.

Step : 5

Prepare the Buttercream While the cakes cool, prepare the buttercream: In a large bowl, beat 1 cup unsalted butter until it becomes pale and fluffy (about 3-5 minutes).
Gradually add 2 cups icing sugar, ½ cup at a time, and continue to beat until fully incorporated and smooth.
Add 1 tsp vanilla extract and beat again to combine.

Step : 6

Chill the Cake For best results, refrigerate the cake for a day before assembling. This will enhance the flavor and make it easier to work with.

Step : 7

Assemble and Frost the Cake.
Spread a thick layer of whipped buttercream between the cake layers.
Frost the entire cake with the remaining buttercream, smoothing it with a spatula.
Garnish the cake with chocolate shavings and cherries, or any other topping of your choice.

Tips: Refrigerate the cake after frosting for at least 2 hours before serving for a firm texture. Serve chilled and enjoy!
